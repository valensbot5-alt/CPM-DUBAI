How Long Do You Need a CPM Machine After Knee Replacement?

The most common question from patients discharged after total knee replacement surgery — answered with precision.

After a total knee replacement (TKR), your surgeon or physiotherapist may prescribe Continuous Passive Motion (CPM) therapy. The machine gently flexes and extends your knee while you rest — but how long should you actually use it?بعد استبدال الركبة الكلي، قد يصف لك جراحك أو معالجك الطبيعي علاج الحركة السلبية المستمرة (CPM). يُحرّك الجهاز ركبتك بلطف أثناء راحتك — لكن كم من الوقت يجب أن تستخدمه فعلاً؟

The standard recommendation is 4 to 6 weeks of daily CPM use post-TKR, typically 4–8 hours per day. However, your surgeon will personalise this based on your pre-surgical range of motion, age, and recovery progress.

The First 48 Hours: Critical Windowأول ٤٨ ساعة: النافذة الحرجة

Research consistently shows that starting CPM therapy within 24–48 hours of surgery produces the best outcomes. Early motion prevents scar tissue from forming in the joint, reduces stiffness, and has been shown to decrease the need for manipulation under anaesthesia (MUA) later.تُظهر الأبحاث باستمرار أن بدء علاج CPM خلال 24-48 ساعة من الجراحة يحقق أفضل النتائج. الحركة المبكرة تمنع تكوّن نسيج ندبي في المفصل، وتقلل التصلب.

This is why many surgeons now request that CPM machines be ready at the patient's home before discharge — so there's no gap in therapy.لهذا السبب يطلب كثير من الجراحين الآن أن يكون جهاز CPM جاهزاً في منزل المريض قبل الخروج من المستشفى.

Week-by-Week CPM Duration Guideدليل مدة استخدام CPM أسبوعاً بأسبوع

Week 1
4–6 hrs/day · 0°–60° flexionStart low. Focus on tolerance. Ice the knee before and after sessions. Pain is expected but should be manageable.
Week 2
6–8 hrs/day · up to 90° flexionYour physiotherapist will advance the angle. Reaching 90° by end of week 2 is a key milestone for most surgeons.
Week 3–4
4–6 hrs/day · 90°–110° flexionAs active exercises begin, CPM time is often reduced. The machine becomes a supplement to physiotherapy rather than the primary treatment.
Week 5–6
1–2 hrs/day · maintenanceMost patients use CPM in the evenings only. Discontinuation is decided by your physiotherapist based on achieved range of motion.

Signs You Should Continue Beyond 6 Weeksعلامات تستدعي الاستمرار لأكثر من ٦ أسابيع

  • You have not yet achieved 90° of flexion
  • Your surgeon has noted increased scar tissue formation
  • You are recovering from a revision knee replacement (which typically requires longer CPM use)
  • Your physiotherapist has identified joint stiffness persisting into week 5

When to Stop Earlierمتى يمكن التوقف مبكراً

Some patients — particularly younger, highly motivated patients who achieve excellent early range of motion — can discontinue CPM after 3–4 weeks with their surgeon's approval. Do not stop on your own initiative. Always consult your physiotherapist before ending CPM therapy.بعض المرضى يمكنهم إيقاف CPM بعد 3-4 أسابيع بموافقة الجراح. لا تتوقف من تلقاء نفسك. استشر معالجك الطبيعي دائماً قبل إنهاء علاج CPM.

Dubai-Specific Consideration: Heat & Inflammation

Patients recovering in Dubai's climate should be aware that heat increases inflammatory response. Keep your recovery room cool (ideally 20–22°C), and always ice your knee for 20 minutes after each CPM session to control swelling.يجب على المرضى في مناخ دبي أن يعلموا أن الحرارة تزيد الاستجابة الالتهابية. حافظ على برودة غرفة التعافي (20-22 درجة مئوية)، وضع الثلج على ركبتك 20 دقيقة بعد كل جلسة CPM.

Week-by-Week Knee Recovery Timeline After TKR Surgery الجدول الزمني لتعافي الركبة بعد جراحة الاستبدال أسبوعاً بأسبوع

From day 1 to week 12 — exactly what to expect at each stage of your total knee replacement recovery.من اليوم الأول حتى الأسبوع الثاني عشر — ما تتوقعه في كل مرحلة من تعافيك.

Total knee replacement (TKR) surgery is one of the most successful procedures in modern orthopaedic medicine — with a 95% satisfaction rate at 10 years. But the recovery demands patience, consistency, and the right tools at the right time.جراحة استبدال الركبة الكلي من أكثر العمليات نجاحاً في طب العظام الحديث — بمعدل رضا 95% بعد 10 سنوات. لكن التعافي يتطلب الصبر والاتساق والأدوات المناسبة.

Every patient recovers differently. This timeline represents the average experience — your surgeon's instructions always take priority over any general guidance.

Days 1–3: Hospital to Homeالأيام 1-3: من المستشفى إلى المنزل

You will be mobilised within 24 hours of surgery with a walking frame or crutches. Expect significant swelling, bruising, and discomfort — this is entirely normal. Before discharge, confirm that your CPM machine is ready at home. Starting CPM within 48 hours is strongly associated with better outcomes.

  • Ice regularly (20 min on, 40 min off)
  • Keep leg elevated above heart level when resting
  • Begin CPM at 0°–40° for 2–4 hours on day 1
  • Take prescribed medication on schedule

Week 1–2: Foundation Phaseالأسبوع 1-2: مرحلة التأسيس

The primary goal is reaching 90° of knee flexion. This is the minimum range needed to walk normally, climb stairs, and sit comfortably.الهدف الرئيسي هو الوصول إلى 90 درجة من ثني الركبة. هذا الحد الأدنى للمشي الطبيعي وصعود الدرج والجلوس بارتياح. علاج CPM يعمل 6-8 ساعات يومياً.

Week 3–4: Active Recovery Beginsالأسبوع 3-4: بدء التعافي النشط

Your physiotherapist will introduce active exercises — quad sets, straight leg raises, and heel slides. CPM use continues but at reduced hours. Most patients begin walking with a single crutch or cane. Driving is still not permitted.يُقدّم معالجك الطبيعي تمارين نشطة — تقلصات الفخذ، رفع الساق، وتمرير الكعب. يستمر CPM بساعات مخفضة. معظم المرضى يبدأون المشي بعكاز واحد. القيادة غير مسموحة بعد.

Week 6: First Major Milestoneالأسبوع 6: أول إنجاز رئيسي

The 6-week post-op appointment typically marks the transition from acute recovery to rehabilitation. Your surgeon will X-ray the joint, assess range of motion, and determine whether CPM therapy should continue. Many patients achieve 110°–120° flexion by this point.

Week 8–12: Return to Daily Lifeالأسبوع 8-12: العودة للحياة اليومية

Most patients resume driving, light household activities, and extended walking. Swelling continues to reduce but may persist up to 6 months. Full recovery — including the ability to kneel, cycle, and play golf — typically takes 9–12 months.معظم المرضى يستأنفون القيادة والأنشطة المنزلية الخفيفة. التورم يستمر في التراجع لكنه قد يبقى حتى 6 أشهر. التعافي الكامل يستغرق عادةً 9-12 شهراً.

CPM Machine vs. Manual Physiotherapy: Which Is Better? جهاز CPM مقابل العلاج الطبيعي اليدوي: أيهما أفضل؟

Clinical studies compared. When CPM alone is sufficient — and when you need both.مقارنة الدراسات السريرية. متى يكفي CPM وحده — ومتى تحتاج الاثنين معاً.

One of the most debated topics in post-surgical knee rehabilitation is whether Continuous Passive Motion (CPM) therapy can replace conventional physiotherapy, or whether both are required.من أكثر الموضوعات نقاشاً في إعادة تأهيل الركبة ما إذا كان علاج CPM يمكنه الحلول محل العلاج الطبيعي التقليدي أم أن كليهما ضروري.

The short answer: they serve different purposes, and the best outcomes come from combining both.الإجابة المختصرة: كلاهما يخدم غرضاً مختلفاً، وأفضل النتائج تأتي من الجمع بينهما.

What CPM Does That Physio Can'tما يفعله CPM ولا يستطيع العلاج الطبيعي فعله

CPM machines work while you sleep, rest, or watch television. They provide continuous, consistent motion for hours at a time — something no physiotherapist can manually replicate. Clinical evidence shows CPM significantly reduces the risk of joint contracture (permanent stiffness) in the first 2 weeks post-surgery, when scar tissue formation is most aggressive.

  • Continuous motion for 4–8 hours uninterrupted
  • Precise, programmable angle control
  • Available 24 hours — no appointment needed
  • Reduces post-op swelling through gentle lymphatic drainage

What Physiotherapy Does That CPM Can'tما يفعله العلاج الطبيعي ولا يستطيع CPM فعله

Manual physiotherapy is active — it builds muscle strength, trains proprioception (balance and spatial awareness), and teaches your brain to control the new joint. CPM is passive; it moves your knee, but it doesn't train your muscles to move it themselves.العلاج الطبيعي اليدوي نشط — يبني قوة العضلات، ويُدرّب الحس العميق، ويُعلّم دماغك التحكم في المفصل الجديد. CPM سلبي؛ يُحرّك ركبتك لكنه لا يُدرّب عضلاتك على تحريكها بنفسها.

The Evidenceالأدلة العلمية

A 2023 meta-analysis of 14 randomised controlled trials found that patients who combined CPM therapy with structured physiotherapy achieved significantly higher range of motion scores at 6 weeks than those who used either intervention alone.وجد تحليل تلوي عام 2023 لـ14 تجربة عشوائية أن المرضى الذين جمعوا بين CPM والعلاج الطبيعي المنظّم حققوا درجات نطاق حركة أعلى بكثير بعد 6 أسابيع مقارنةً باستخدام أي منهما منفرداً.

Recommendation: Use CPM for the first 4–6 weeks post-surgery, and begin structured physiotherapy as soon as your surgeon clears it — typically within the first week.

Managing Pain & Swelling at Home After Knee Surgery إدارة الألم والتورم في المنزل بعد جراحة الركبة

Proven techniques for reducing swelling, managing pain naturally, and knowing when to call your doctor.تقنيات مثبتة لتقليل التورم، وإدارة الألم طبيعياً، ومعرفة متى تتصل بطبيبك.

Post-surgical knee swelling and pain are your body's normal healing response. The goal isn't to eliminate them entirely — it's to keep them at a manageable level so you can complete your CPM therapy and physiotherapy sessions effectively.

The RICE Protocol (Still Works)بروتوكول RICE (لا يزال فعّالاً)

  • Rest — limit weight-bearing to what your surgeon has approved
  • Ice — 20 minutes on, 40 minutes off. Never ice directly on skin
  • Compression — wear your compression stocking as instructed, especially when up
  • Elevation — leg above heart level reduces swelling fastest

Ice Timing Relative to CPMتوقيت الثلج بالنسبة لجلسات CPM

The most effective routine is to ice your knee for 20 minutes before each CPM session (to reduce inflammation and improve tolerance) and again for 20 minutes after (to manage the increased circulation).الروتين الأكثر فعالية هو وضع الثلج 20 دقيقة قبل كل جلسة CPM (لتقليل الالتهاب وتحسين التحمّل) ومجدداً 20 دقيقة بعدها (لإدارة زيادة الدورة الدموية).

Medication: Follow the Scheduleالأدوية: اتبع الجدول الزمني

Anti-inflammatory medication works best when taken consistently on schedule — not just when pain becomes severe. If your surgeon has prescribed a course of NSAIDs or paracetamol, take them at the prescribed intervals even when pain feels tolerable. Skipping doses allows inflammation to build up and makes the next CPM session much harder.

Warning Signs That Need Immediate Attentionعلامات تحذيرية تستدعي اهتماماً فورياً

  • Sudden increase in swelling, particularly if one-sided and hot
  • Calf pain or tightness (possible DVT)
  • Fever above 38.5°C
  • Wound discharge, redness, or opening
  • Chest pain or shortness of breath
If you experience calf pain with redness or warmth, go to A&E immediately. Deep vein thrombosis is a serious but preventable complication of knee surgery.

CPM Machine Settings Explained: Angle, Speed & Duration شرح إعدادات جهاز CPM: الزاوية، السرعة والمدة

Understanding your CPM settings helps you use it more effectively and safely.فهم إعدادات جهاز CPM يساعدك على استخدامه بفعالية أكبر وأمان أعلى.

When our team delivers your CPM machine, we configure it according to your surgeon or physiotherapist's prescription. But understanding what each setting does — and why it matters — helps you use the machine more effectively and flag any issues early.عندما يُوصّل فريقنا جهاز CPM الخاص بك، نُهيّئه وفق وصفة جراحك أو معالجك الطبيعي. لكن فهم ما يفعله كل إعداد يساعدك على استخدام الجهاز بفعالية أكبر.

Flexion Angle (Maximum Bend)زاوية الثني (الحد الأقصى للانحناء)

This is the most important setting. It determines how far your knee bends during each cycle. Your starting angle is set conservatively (typically 40°–60°) and should be advanced gradually — usually 5–10° every 1–2 days — under physiotherapy guidance. Never advance the angle yourself without authorisation from your physiotherapist.

Extension Angle (Full Straightening)زاوية المد (التمديد الكامل)

The extension setting controls how straight your knee gets at the end of each cycle. For most TKR patients, the target is full extension (0°). If your machine doesn't bring your knee to full extension, inform your physiotherapist — residual flexion contracture is a significant complication.

Speedالسرعة

CPM machines typically have 3–5 speed settings. Slower speeds are used in the first few days when pain and swelling are highest. Speed can be increased as you become more comfortable. If a particular speed causes pain beyond a 4/10 on your pain scale, reduce it and inform your physiotherapist.

Duration Per Sessionمدة كل جلسة

Sessions of 2–4 hours are most common. Longer than 4 hours in a single session can cause skin irritation at the contact points — ensure padding is correctly positioned and check your skin after each use.

Always contact your KneeCare support team via WhatsApp if you're unsure about any setting. Never adjust the machine beyond your prescribed parameters.

The Best Foods to Eat During Knee Recovery أفضل الأطعمة خلال تعافي الركبة

Anti-inflammatory foods, protein for tissue repair, and supplements that actually work.الأطعمة المضادة للالتهابات، والبروتين لإصلاح الأنسجة، والمكملات الفعّالة فعلاً.

Nutrition is one of the most under-discussed aspects of knee surgery recovery. What you eat directly affects inflammation levels, tissue repair speed, and muscle recovery.التغذية من أكثر جوانب تعافي الركبة إهمالاً. ما تأكله يؤثر مباشرةً على مستويات الالتهاب، وسرعة إصلاح الأنسجة، وتعافي العضلات.

Protein: The Most Important Macronutrientالبروتين: أهم العناصر الغذائية الكبرى

Surgery is a catabolic event — your body breaks down muscle tissue to fuel the healing process. Without sufficient dietary protein, your muscles will weaken significantly during recovery. Aim for 1.5–2g of protein per kg of body weight daily during the first 6 weeks. Good sources include chicken, fish, eggs, Greek yogurt, and legumes.

Anti-Inflammatory Foods to Prioritiseالأطعمة المضادة للالتهابات التي يجب إعطاؤها الأولوية

  • Oily fish (salmon, sardines) — omega-3 fatty acids reduce joint inflammation
  • Turmeric — curcumin has clinically demonstrated anti-inflammatory properties
  • Leafy greens (spinach, kale) — rich in Vitamin K, essential for bone health
  • Berries — antioxidants reduce oxidative stress from surgery
  • Olive oil — oleocanthal has anti-inflammatory effects similar to ibuprofen

Foods That Increase Inflammation (Avoid)الأطعمة التي تزيد الالتهاب (تجنّبها)

  • Refined sugars and white flour products
  • Processed and fried foods
  • Alcohol (also interacts with pain medication)
  • Excess red meat, especially processed meats

Supplements Worth Consideringمكملات تستحق الدراسة

Vitamin D and calcium support bone integration of the implant. Collagen peptides (10g/day) have emerging evidence for supporting connective tissue repair. Magnesium supports muscle recovery and sleep quality — both critical during rehabilitation. Always consult your surgeon before starting any supplement.

Hydration Matters More Than You Thinkالترطيب أهم مما تظن

Adequate hydration supports lymphatic drainage (which reduces swelling), maintains cartilage health, and improves medication absorption. Aim for 2.5–3 litres of water daily, more if you are in Dubai's warm climate.

5 Safe Exercises to Do Alongside Your CPM Machine ٥ تمارين آمنة لممارستها جانب جهاز CPM الخاص بك

Gentle exercises approved for post-surgical patients that complement CPM therapy and speed up range of motion recovery.تمارين لطيفة معتمدة للمرضى بعد الجراحة تكمل علاج CPM وتسرّع تعافي نطاق الحركة.

CPM therapy moves your knee passively — which is essential in the early weeks. But pairing it with gentle active exercises trains your muscles to control the motion themselves, accelerating the transition from CPM-dependent to fully independent movement.علاج CPM يُحرّك ركبتك بشكل سلبي — وهو ضروري في الأسابيع الأولى. لكن إقرانه بتمارين نشطة لطيفة يُدرّب عضلاتك على التحكم بالحركة بنفسها.

Always get your surgeon's clearance before starting any exercise programme. These exercises are general guidance only and should be confirmed with your physiotherapist.

1. Ankle Pumps (Day 1 Onwards)١. ضخ الكاحل (من اليوم الأول)

While lying flat, flex and point your feet repeatedly — 10 reps, every hour you're awake. This activates the calf muscle pump, reducing the risk of DVT and improving circulation to the surgical site. Simple, safe, and critically important.

2. Quad Sets (Day 2 Onwards)٢. تقلصات الفخذ (من اليوم الثاني)

Lying flat with your leg straight, tighten your thigh muscle (quadriceps) by pressing the back of your knee towards the bed. Hold 5 seconds, release. 3 sets of 10 daily. This maintains quad activation without putting any load on the new joint.

3. Straight Leg Raises (Week 1–2)٣. رفع الساق المستقيمة (الأسبوع 1-2)

With your operated leg straight and your other knee bent, tighten your quad and lift your straight leg to the height of your other knee. Hold 2 seconds, lower slowly. If you cannot lift without the knee bending, focus on quad sets for another few days first. 3 sets of 10.

4. Heel Slides (Week 1–2)٤. تمرير الكعب (الأسبوع 1-2)

Lying flat, slowly slide your heel towards your buttocks, bending the knee as far as comfort allows. This is the active equivalent of your CPM machine — it trains your muscles to initiate the motion. Hold at maximum comfortable bend for 5 seconds. 3 sets of 10.

5. Short Arc Quads (Week 2–3)٥. تمرين الفخذ القوسي القصير (الأسبوع 2-3)

Place a rolled towel under your knee to support it at around 40° flexion. From this position, slowly straighten the leg fully. Hold 2 seconds, lower slowly. This exercise is critical for achieving full extension — one of the most important functional milestones in TKR recovery.

Timing Relative to CPMالتوقيت بالنسبة لجلسات CPM

The ideal sequence is: complete your CPM session → rest 30 minutes → perform your exercises. Your knee will be warmed up from the CPM session, making active exercises easier and more productive. Follow with 20 minutes of ice.التسلسل المثالي: أكمل جلسة CPM → استرح 30 دقيقة → نفّذ تمارينك. ركبتك ستكون دافئة من جلسة CPM مما يجعل التمارين أسهل وأكثر إنتاجية. ثم ضع الثلج 20 دقيقة.
